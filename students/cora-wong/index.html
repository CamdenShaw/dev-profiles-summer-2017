One of my best travel experience was my post-graduation trip to Orlando, Florida. No more finals! No more homeworks! My schoolmates and I planned the whole trip by ourselves. None of us have been there. It was an adventure for all of us too. Eight of us took an 8 hours flight from Vancouver to Orlando. It didn't have any direct flights at that time. Even though, the flight was long, it was worth it because Orlando is a magical place. We went to Walt Disney World, the largest theme park in the world as well as Universal Studios Florida, I am a big fan of Harry Potter. We went to the beaches, tried many different food and had a lot of fun. Travelling doesn't have to be expensive, what is most important is the poeple you travel with that makes most different.
