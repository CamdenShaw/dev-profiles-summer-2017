Name Description Category
Ghost The best blogging platform in the world. Hands down. I use a local installation with bitnami as a journal. CMS
Parsehub Awesome GUI for building web scrapers. Turn the whole web into an API and dynamically extract data from any website. Great freemium access to a useful tool. Data Science
Snipcart A flexible e-commerce solution with a fully customizable HTML/Javascript shopping cart and dashboard. Add products directly in your markup with attribute tags. Plays nice with Ghost. E-commerce
RescueTime Time logging application. Automatically tracks and categorizes your time spent on your computer. Shows a productivity score, custom reports for deeper insights. A nice accountability tool. Productivity
Habitica Awesome to-do list application combined with gamification features. Level-up a pixelated avatar while accomplishing your real life goals. Productivity
Provenance A systems emulator for iOS. Play classic GameBoy, Super Nintendo, Sega and Atari games on your iPhone. Can be pretty brutal on your battery life but it's WORTH IT. Essentials
